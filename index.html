Reading: Account of a Visit from St. Nicholas, the Original (1823) Version of 'Twas the Night Before Christmas

This is my reading of the original poem known commonly today as 'Twas the Night Before Christmas. The original text has been neither modernized nor bowdlerized. So, if you listen carefully, you'll hear things like St. Nick refering to Donder and Blitzen by their original names: Dunder and Blixem; and wishing the world a "happy", not "merry" Christmas.

Enjoy...and have a "Happy Christmas".
